Welcome to Our Hotel

We are a family of food lovers who wanted to share our passion for traditional South Indian cuisine with the world.
Our journey began with a small tea stall in a quaint village, where we served freshly brewed tea and delicious idlis to the locals.
As our popularity grew, we expanded our menu to include coffee, pongal, and other regional specialties.

About Us

Hotel Sri Durga Bhavan is a humble attempt to recreate the magic of traditional South Indian cuisine, passed down through generations of our ancestors. Our founder, Chef Saravanan, drew inspiration from his childhood memories of his mother's cooking and his grandfather's stories of ancient Tamil cuisine. With a passion to share this rich culinary heritage with the world, he established Saiva Unavagam, where every dish is crafted with love and care, using only the freshest ingredients and time-honored techniques. Our restaurant is a tribute to the rich cultural legacy of South India, and we invite you to join us on this culinary journey.
